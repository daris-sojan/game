Rules

  • Use arrow keys to move up, down, left, and right through the maze.
  • Press Enter to generate a new maze.
  • Press Shift to go to the previous level.
  • Complete 3 mazes to advance to the next level.

Sound Cue

  • Goal sound: As you get closer to the goal, the background music gets louder.
